Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register for an account, modify your profile, or use the CBMS Pro API. This includes your email address, securely hashed passwords, and OAuth IDs (if registering via Google or Microsoft).

2. File Uploads & Processing

When you upload Excel documents (.xlsx, .xls) for bulk reporting, the data is processed locally within our secure backend pipeline to orchestrate message delivery. We do not permanently store your contact lists unless explicitly requested via a CRM integration feature.

3. Security Metrics

We utilize HttpOnly JSON Web Tokens (JWT) to track your active session and prevent Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) attacks. Your connection to the server is encrypted, and your API keys are treated as highly sensitive data payloads.
